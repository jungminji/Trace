# Assitive Technology

- An umbrella term for a broad range of devices, software and tools that help any person with a disability complete a task.



- Examples of assitive devices
  - Screen reader
  - Braille display
  - Magnification
  - Voice control
  - Switch access
  - Sip and Puff
  - Eye tracking
